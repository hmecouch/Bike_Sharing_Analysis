# Citi Bike Analysis


## Overview


Citi Bike is a bike share program with bikes locked into a network of docking stations all over the NYC area. It's now the largest bike sharing program in the nation, boasting a fleet of 27,000 bikes and 1,700 stations across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, Jersey City, and Hoboken. This system provides New Yorkers and visitors with an additional transportation option that is fun, efficient, environmentally friendly and convenient. 

Citi Bike data can provide valuable insights for entrepreneurs considering starting a bike sharing program in their city. The analysis should include factors such as usage patterns, peak times, revenue, and customer demographics. This information can be used to determine the feasibility and potential profitability of a bike sharing program in a specific location. The data should be analyzed carefully and thoroughly to draw accurate conclusions and make informed decisions for potential investors.

## Summary


Our data analysis has revealed some intriguing insights into the usage of bike-sharing in New York City. Our findings indicate that there is a significant demand for bike-sharing services in that market, particularly among male commuters. The busiest usage times during the week are during the typical rush hours of 7am-9am and 4pm-7pm, suggesting that the bikes are being utilized for commuting purposes.

Furthermore, our data includes two heat maps that show the top starting locations and top ending locations in New York City. This information can be extremely valuable in determining where bike-sharing infrastructure should be deployed in order to best serve the market demand.

Now, considering Des Moines, the market demand for bike-sharing services may differ, however, we believe that the findings from New York City provide a useful benchmark for comparison. Based on these results, there is potential for a bike-sharing company in Des Moines to serve a similar demographic and target market. By utilizing the insights from the starting and ending location heat maps, we can optimize the deployment of our bike-sharing infrastructure in Des Moines to best meet the needs of the market. These insights highlight the potential for strong returns on investment in Des Moines
